Tips and tricks:

  • Make sure to have the subject as close to you as possible and is at the center of your camera before you capture for a 3 star-rated photo, as you will acquire less money for a 2-star or 1-star photograph. Crouching is a good way to get closer to your subject, depending on what it is (ex: "Disturbed Salt", "Burnt Cross").
  • Ensure to take a picture of the Umbra Board before use, as a broken Umbra Board will no longer count as a Cursed Object in photographs.
  • Use the Summoning Circle as an easy way to get a "Ghost" photo during the few seconds of the ghost manifesting and before it starts the Cursed Hunt. Similarly, ensure to take a picture of the Summoning Circle before use, as the photograph may recognize a photo of the Ghost as a photo of a "Cursed Object" instead.
  • Closing the closet door is purely optional as being in the locker/closet already guarantees your safety (unless it is a Vesper). Seize the opportunity to take a photo!

💬 Roblox Chat Commands

Outside of hunts, asking these questions in the Roblox chat has a 20% chance of succeeding, each with a separate 20 seconds countdown.

Command Effect
"Show yourself" The ghost will become visible for a few seconds, allowing for a quick photo.
"Give me a sign" The ghost will cause the lights in the current room to flicker only if the current room is the ghost room.
"Can you write in the book" The ghost will write in the Spirit Book if they have the corresponding evidence.

⚔️ Difficulty

In Demonology, party leaders can adjust the difficulty of the job which can range from Easy to Nightmare. Each difficulty has its own reward multiplier for each successful job where easier difficulties have a lower multiplier compared to harder difficulties. Some game mechanics may be enhanced or reduced based on the difficulty.

Easy

Easy Mode is the beginner difficulty in the game as it is modified to be easier than Medium. This difficulty comes with a higher chance to gain evidence, little ghost hunts, but has the smallest reward multiplier.

  • 1x rewards
  • Slower Energy drain
  • Ghosts will hunt when the players average energy reaches ≤ 25%
  • Higher frequency of evidence-based interactions (ex: Laser Projector, Freezing Temperatures, Ghost Writing, etc.)
  • Fuse box is automatically powered on
  • All ghosts will stop hunting after a kill

Medium

Medium Mode is likely the default version of the game with no aspects increased or decreased, besides the increase of rewards from Easy Mode.

  • 1.25x rewards
  • Ghosts will hunt when the players average energy reaches ≤ 50%

Hard

Hard Mode is the difficulty for more experienced people trying to reach a minimum challenge or more rewards.

  • 1.5x rewards
  • Ghosts will hunt when the players average energy reaches ≤ 75%

Nightmare

Nightmare Mode is the toughest difficulty in the game excluding runs that are customized to be more difficult. This difficulty comes with dangerous ghost hunts, higher ghost aggression, and a more difficult time to gather evidence. This difficulty was revamped in V1.1.0.

  • 2x rewards
  • No setup duration
  • Higher hunt frequency, longer hunt durations (36.5s), no grace period, and decreased hunt cooldowns (25s)
  • Start at 75% energy, below hunt threshold (85%)
  • Energy Monitor is broken
  • Energy Drinks regen 30% energy
  • Ghost Room changes over time
  • Evidence-specific changes:
    • Handprints last for 60 seconds instead of 120 seconds
    • Wither reflects on the site after 8 minutes instead of 3 minutes
  • Higher frequency of aggression-based interactions (ex: breaking glass, throwing objects, etc.)
  • No Cursed Objects

Tip: Drinking one Energy Drink after starting the job gives you about 100 seconds before a hunt can start.

In v1.10.0, the maximum multiplier for custom is 4.07x.
